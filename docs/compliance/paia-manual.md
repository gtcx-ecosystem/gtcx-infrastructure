## 1. Introduction

GTCX respects the right of access to information as enshrined in the Constitution of South Africa and PAIA. This manual is published in compliance with Section 14 of PAIA and outlines the categories of records held by GTCX, the procedures for requesting access, and the fees applicable.

## 4. Request Procedure

### 4.1 How to Request Access

1. Complete the PAIA request form (available at `https://gtcx.io/paia-form`).
2. Submit the form to **dpo@gtcx.io** with subject line: **PAIA Request — [Your Name] — [Date]**.
3. Include proof of identity (ID or passport) and, if acting on behalf of another, proof of authority.

### 4.2 Response Timeline

| Step                                                           | Timeline                                   |
| -------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| Acknowledgement                                                | 5 business days                            |
| Decision (grant / refuse / defer)                              | 30 days from receipt                       |
| Extension (if voluminous or third-party consultation required) | +30 days (notified within initial 30 days) |
| Internal appeal (if refused)                                   | 30 days from refusal notice                |
| External appeal (to Information Regulator)                     | 180 days from refusal notice               |

### 4.3 Fees

| Service                            | Fee (ZAR)   |
| ---------------------------------- | ----------- |
| Request submission                 | Free        |
| Search and preparation (per hour)  | R150        |
| Photocopying / printing (per page) | R2          |
| Electronic copy (USB / email)      | R50         |
| Postage / courier                  | Actual cost |

**Fee waiver:** Requests made by persons requesting access to their own personal information are free of charge.

## 5. Grounds for Refusal

GTCX may refuse access in terms of PAIA Chapter 4, including:

- **Section 34:** Mandatory protection of privacy of a third party.
- **Section 36:** Mandatory protection of commercial information.
- **Section 37:** Mandatory protection of confidential information of a third party.
- **Section 41:** Protection of safety of individuals and protection of property.
- **Section 42:** Protection of law enforcement and legal proceedings.
- **Section 44:** Defence, security, and international relations.

## 6. POPIA Integration

This PAIA manual is published in conjunction with GTCX's POPIA compliance framework. All requests for personal information are also processed under POPIA Chapter 5 (Rights of Data Subjects). Where PAIA and POPIA overlap, the stricter protection standard applies.

## 7. Updates

This manual is reviewed annually and updated within 30 days of:

- A change in contact details.
- A significant change in record categories.
- A court or regulator ruling affecting access rights.
