# POL-07: Human Resource Security

> **Status:** Current
> **Date:** 2026-05-10
> **Owner:** GTCX Infrastructure

**Annex A Reference:** A.7 — People Controls
**Effective Date:** 2026-05-08
**Review Cycle:** Annual
**Owner:** Head of HR (co-owned with CISO)
**Approved By:** Board Security Committee

## 1. Purpose

Ensure that personnel and contractors understand their security responsibilities and are suitable for their roles throughout the employment lifecycle.

## 2. Scope

All GTCX employees, contractors, interns, and temporary staff from pre-employment through termination.

## 3. Policy Statement

1. **Pre-employment screening.** Background verification is completed before granting system access. Screening includes identity verification, reference checks, and criminal record checks where legally permitted. For roles with access to financial data or cryptographic keys, enhanced screening is required.

2. **Terms of employment.** All personnel sign a confidentiality/NDA agreement and an acceptable use policy before receiving credentials. Contracts explicitly state security responsibilities and consequences of policy violations.

3. **Security awareness.** All personnel complete security awareness training within 14 days of onboarding and annually thereafter. Training covers phishing, social engineering, data handling, and incident reporting. Completion is tracked; non-compliance triggers access suspension after 30 days.

4. **Disciplinary process.** Violations of security policy are subject to a formal disciplinary process documented in the employee handbook. Severity is assessed by the CISO and HR jointly.

5. **Termination procedures.** Upon termination or role change: all access is revoked within 4 hours, equipment is returned within 24 hours, knowledge transfer is documented, and exit interviews include a security debrief. The offboarding checklist is mandatory for all departures.

## 4. Responsibilities

| Role          | Responsibility                                              |
| ------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| HR            | Execute screening, manage training records, run offboarding |
| CISO          | Define training content, review access revocation           |
| Managers      | Ensure team compliance with training, initiate offboarding  |
| All Personnel | Complete training, report security concerns                 |

## 5. Exceptions

No exceptions to pre-employment screening. Training deadline extensions require CISO approval (maximum 14 additional days).

## 6. Review

Reviewed annually. Training content updated quarterly to reflect the current threat landscape.
