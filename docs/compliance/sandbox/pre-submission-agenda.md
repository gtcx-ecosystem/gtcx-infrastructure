# Pre-Submission Meeting Agenda — Regulatory Sandbox

**Purpose:** Informal meeting with the regulator's sandbox team before formal application  
**Duration:** 45 minutes  
**Date:** ******\_\_\_******  
**Location:** ******\_\_\_******  
**Attendees:** [GTCX team], [Regulator sandbox team]

---

## 1. Introduction (10 minutes)

- **Who we are.** GTCX is a trade compliance platform for African commodity markets. We provide digital identity verification, tamper-evident audit trails, and automated compliance checks for commodity transactions.
- **What we are building.** A platform that replaces paper-based trade compliance with real-time digital verification — KYC, provenance tracking, and AML/CFT screening — purpose-built for African regulatory environments.
- **Why the sandbox.** We want to operate within the regulatory framework from day one. The sandbox gives us a controlled environment to demonstrate compliance before full licensing.
- **Our stage.** Pre-revenue. Platform is built and in testnet. No live customer transactions yet.

---

## 2. Questions for the Sandbox Team (20 minutes)

### Application Process

- What format do you prefer for the application package? (PDF bundle, online portal, physical submission?)
- Is there a template or checklist for the application that we should follow?
- Can we submit iteratively (draft for feedback, then final), or is it a single formal submission?

### Technical Evidence

- What level of technical detail is expected in the architecture documentation?
- What security evidence is required? (Penetration test report? SOC 2? ISO 27001 certificate? Self-assessment?)
- Are there approved penetration testing firms you recommend or require?
- Do you require a live demo, or is documentation sufficient at the application stage?

### Data and Operations

- Are there specific data residency requirements beyond what is in the Data Protection Act?
- Do you require a local data center, or is a regional cloud provider (AWS Cape Town) acceptable?
- Are there capital or insurance requirements for sandbox participants?

### Timeline and Process

- What is the typical review timeline from submission to decision?
- How long does the sandbox period last?
- What are the conditions for graduating from sandbox to full license?
- Is there a fee for the sandbox application?

---

## 3. What We Will Bring (5 minutes)

- **Architecture diagram.** Printed copy of the system architecture overview showing security boundaries, data flow, and encryption.
- **Testnet demo.** Live walkthrough of the platform on testnet — a sample commodity transaction from KYC through compliance check to audit trail.
- **Incident Response Plan summary.** One-page summary of our IRP showing severity classification, escalation matrix, and regulator notification timelines.
- **Jurisdiction configuration.** How the platform adapts retention periods, data residency, and compliance rules per jurisdiction (code-driven, not manual).

---

## 4. Follow-Up Actions (10 minutes)

- Confirm application format and any templates to use
- Confirm security evidence requirements
- Agree on next meeting date (if applicable)
- GTCX to send thank-you email within 24 hours summarizing:
  - What was discussed
  - Action items with owners and deadlines
  - Any documents requested by the sandbox team
  - Proposed timeline for formal submission
