## Role Summary

The Editor-in-Chief is responsible for the editorial integrity, quality, and voice of all [Organization Name] content. This role shapes how [the audience] understands [coverage domain].

---

## Key Responsibilities

### Editorial Leadership

- Set editorial direction and priorities across all platforms
- Maintain consistent voice and quality standards
- Make final decisions on sensitive content
- Represent [Organization Name] publicly as editorial authority

### Quality Control

- Establish and enforce editorial standards
- Review high-stakes content before publication
- Approve breaking news and market-moving intelligence
- Maintain editorial independence from commercial pressures

### Team Development

- Recruit and develop editorial talent
- Build desk editor capabilities
- Create training programs for analysts
- Foster culture of accuracy and insight

### Strategic Planning

- Plan content calendar and coverage priorities
- Identify new coverage areas and opportunities
- Collaborate with intelligence on research agenda
- Partner with commercial on content monetization
