```text
Transaction Graph

```

## Non-Negotiable Product Decisions

1. **Capital Discovery and Formation Intelligence is the primary commercial
   engine.** GTCX must identify and convert the right investors, not merely list
   credible deals.
2. **Mandate acceptance starts confidential pre-market immediately.**
3. **No deal goes live cold.** Default 30-day admission requires at least 25%
   qualified soft-circle coverage; 14-day and 7-day windows ordinarily require
   at least 50% and 75–100%.
4. **Live means close-ready.** The live market is a final diligence, price,
   allocation, approval, settlement, and closing event.
5. **Bankability is a configurable dependency graph.** LOIs, MOUs, offtake,
   insurance, guarantees, agencies, equipment, infrastructure, incentives, and
   conditional support must be encoded with real strength and conversion paths.
6. **Government and institutional signals are first-class.** Ministries,
   regulators, agencies, chambers, communities, and authorities can issue
   precisely scoped, verifiable signals without false endorsement claims.
7. **Independent assurance is modular and portable.** Legal, valuation, model,
   technical, bankability, rating, settlement, and monitoring conclusions
   remain separate.
8. **AI performs production work; accountable humans retain consequential and
   regulated authority.**
9. **GTCX-native systems form the product core.** External parties supply
   adopted authority, professional accountability, proprietary data, and
   execution rails through typed adapters.
10. **Tokenization does not create legality, demand, liquidity, or settlement
    finality.** It is one controlled ownership mechanism.

## First Proof

The first proof should demonstrate:

- a deeply researched investor universe and approved Capital Formation Strategy;
- a complete bankability configuration with support dependencies and Plans
  A/B/C;
- current mandatory authority and independent assurance signals;
- evidence-backed qualified soft-circle coverage before live admission;
- a 14- or 30-day live close to a declared terminal standard;
- reconciled cash, ownership, obligations, and receipts; and
- post-close monitoring and a credible liquidity or exit path.

## Acceptance Evidence

- Fabric outbound acknowledgment and programme identifier.
- Cross-repo ownership and dependency graph.
- Executable epics and first-deal workplan.
- Explicit authority, assurance, and external-dependency register.
- No loss of active tokenization, EIX, UI, regulatory-adapter, or settlement
  work.

## What This Document Does NOT Cover

- Authorization to execute legal, regulatory, government, professional,
  investment, funding, settlement, custody, or ownership actions.
- Confirmation that any external institution, authority, investor, or provider
  has committed to participate.
- Replacement of the canonical specifications and handoff linked above.
