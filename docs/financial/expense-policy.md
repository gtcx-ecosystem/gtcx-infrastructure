# Expense Policy

**Document ID:** GTCX-EXP-001  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** 2026-05-25  
**Owner:** Chief Financial Officer

---

## 1. Purpose

Establish clear guidelines for reimbursable business expenses, approval hierarchies, documentation requirements, and spending limits.

## 2. General Principles

1. **Necessary and reasonable** — Expenses must be directly related to GTCX business.
2. **Pre-approval** — Expenses > $500 require pre-approval except in emergencies.
3. **Documentation** — All expenses require original receipts or invoices.
4. **Timely submission** — Expense reports submitted within 30 days of incurring.
5. **No personal benefit** — Expenses must not confer personal benefit to the employee or their family.

## 3. Approval Hierarchy

| Expense Amount | Pre-Approver    | Final Approver    | SLA              |
| -------------- | --------------- | ----------------- | ---------------- |
| ≤ $100         | Direct manager  | —                 | 3 business days  |
| $100–$500      | Direct manager  | Department head   | 5 business days  |
| $500–$2,000    | Department head | CFO               | 7 business days  |
| $2,000–$10,000 | CFO             | CEO               | 10 business days |
| > $10,000      | CEO             | Board (if > $25K) | 14 business days |

## 4. Per-Diem and Travel

### 4.1 Domestic Travel (South Africa)

| Category                  | Limit (ZAR)   | Notes                                      |
| ------------------------- | ------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| Accommodation (per night) | 2,500         | Economy/business hotels                    |
| Meals (per day)           | 800           | Breakfast 200, Lunch 250, Dinner 350       |
| Ground transport          | Actual cost   | Uber / rental car with justification       |
| Flights                   | Economy class | Business class > 4 hours with CFO approval |

### 4.2 International Travel

| Category                  | Limit (USD)   | Notes                                      |
| ------------------------- | ------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| Accommodation (per night) | 200           | Mid-range hotels                           |
| Meals (per day)           | 100           | Per diem                                   |
| Ground transport          | Actual cost   | Public transit preferred                   |
| Flights                   | Economy class | Business class > 8 hours with CEO approval |

## 5. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are **not** reimbursable:

- Personal entertainment (streaming, gaming, gym).
- Alcohol (except at approved client dinners, limited to 2 drinks).
- Traffic fines and parking tickets.
- Personal vehicle maintenance or fuel for commuting.
- Gifts > $100 per recipient per occasion (requires CEO approval).
- Club memberships (unless directly client-facing and pre-approved).

## 6. Documentation Requirements

| Expense Type             | Required Documentation                                      |
| ------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| All expenses             | Itemized receipt with date, vendor, amount, description     |
| Travel                   | Itinerary + boarding passes + accommodation invoice         |
| Meals (with clients)     | Receipt + attendee list + business purpose                  |
| Software / subscriptions | Invoice + business justification                            |
| Training / conferences   | Agenda + registration confirmation + completion certificate |

## 7. Corporate Card

Employees with corporate cards must:

- Use the card for all business expenses > $50.
- Submit expense reports within 14 days of statement close.
- Never use the card for personal expenses.
- Report lost/stolen cards within 2 hours.

## 8. Violations

| Violation                           | Consequence                                    |
| ----------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Missing receipts (> 3 instances)    | Reimbursement delayed; manager review          |
| Personal expenses on corporate card | Immediate card suspension; disciplinary action |
| Expense fraud                       | Termination + legal action                     |
| Repeated policy breaches            | Performance review + potential termination     |
