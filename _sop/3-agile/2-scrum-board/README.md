## What Belongs Here

- Sprint plans and sprint tracking files
- Epic and feature breakdowns
- Backlog items (MoSCoW or priority-ordered)
- Test plans and UAT results
- Retrospective notes and action items

Do not put executive summaries or product briefs here. Those belong in `1-overview/`.
