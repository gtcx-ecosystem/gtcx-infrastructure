# Protocol: Audit Standard

## Version

1.0

## Summary

Defines minimum requirements for any audit in the [Organization Name] ecosystem.

## Audit Requirements

- Scope and time window are explicit
- Evidence is recorded with paths or sources
- Severity is assigned consistently
- Action items have owners and deadlines

## Required Sections (All Audits)

- Executive summary
- Scope and methodology
- Findings with evidence
- Severity and impact assessment
- Recommendations and owners
