## Frequently Asked Questions

### Billing & Accounts

**Can I switch plans?**
Yes. Upgrade anytime and we'll prorate. Downgrade at the end of your billing period.

**Do you offer refunds?**
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on first subscriptions. After that, no refunds but you keep access through your billing period.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and wire transfer for annual Institutional plans.

**Do you offer discounts for NGOs or academic institutions?**
Yes. Contact us for our impact pricing program (up to 50% off).

### Access & Features

**How many people can use one subscription?**
Free, Briefing, and Professional are single-seat licenses. Institutional includes unlimited seats for your organization.

**Can I share my login?**
No. Single-seat licenses are for individual use. We monitor for account sharing and may suspend accounts that violate terms.

**What happens to my data if I cancel?**
You lose access to the platform immediately upon cancellation (or at end of billing period for downgrades). We retain your account data for 90 days in case you resubscribe.

### Enterprise

**Can we get a trial before committing to Institutional?**
Yes. We offer 30-day pilots for qualified organizations. Contact sales.

**Do you offer multi-year discounts?**
Yes. 10% discount for 2-year commitments, 15% for 3-year.

**Can we white-label reports for our clients?**
Yes, with Institutional subscription. Custom branding available for reports.
