## What belongs here

- Domain glossary and terminology definitions
- Market research and competitive analysis
- User-facing documentation (GitBook content)
- Finalized published research and whitepapers
- Release notes and version history
- Archived or historical documentation

## What does NOT belong here

- Active architecture docs (→ `4-engineering/`)
- Product specs (→ `6-specs/`)
- Operational runbooks (→ `5-devops/`)
