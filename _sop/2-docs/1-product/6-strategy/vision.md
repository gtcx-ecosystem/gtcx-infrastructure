## IV. Editorial Principles

### Authority Through Precision

We lead with conclusions, not throat-clearing. Every claim is quantified. Vague language is forbidden.

**Bad:** "[Thing] increased significantly"
**Good:** "[Thing] rose [X]% — [context that makes it meaningful]"

### Precision Over Approximation

Named sources over anonymous attribution. Specific [markets / jurisdictions], not broad generalizations. Declared methodology over black-box analysis. Quantified outcomes over qualitative assessments. Every claim traceable to data.

### Independence Is Non-Negotiable

Editorial decisions are made by editors, not commercial teams. We do not trade favorable coverage for access or revenue. We do not soften findings for advertisers, partners, or governments.

### Original Research First

We develop primary sources, conduct original analysis, and build proprietary datasets. Aggregation is not intelligence. If we cannot add original insight, we do not publish.

### Stakeholder Perspective

Every analysis considers implications for multiple stakeholders. Single-perspective analysis is incomplete analysis.

### Verification Rigor

Multiple source confirmation. Clear attribution. Uncertainty acknowledged. Confidence levels indicated. Methodology published. Data sources disclosed.
