# Pricing

> [Organization Name] pricing strategy and structure.

---

## Pricing Philosophy

### Core Principles

1. **Value-based pricing** — Price reflects value delivered, not cost to produce
2. **Accessibility tiers** — Free tier ensures market development and pipeline
3. **Geographic sensitivity** — Adjusted pricing where purchasing power differs significantly
4. **Transparency** — Clear pricing, no hidden fees
5. **Annual incentives** — Discounts for annual commitment
