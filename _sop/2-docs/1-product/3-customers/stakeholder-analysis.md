# Stakeholder Analysis

> A map of all parties who have a stake in [Organization Name]'s success — including non-customers.

---

## Overview

Stakeholders are not limited to paying customers. They include anyone who can influence or be affected by [Organization Name]'s operations, products, or outcomes. Understanding all stakeholders — and designing appropriate engagement for each — is critical to sustainable market entry.

---

```
                        HIGH INFLUENCE

            ┌─────────────────┼─────────────────┐
            │                 │                 │
     LOW    │   [Manage:      │  [Manage         │   HIGH

            │   Carefully]    │                 │
            │                 │                 │
            ├─────────────────┼─────────────────┤
            │                 │                 │
            │   [Keep         │  [Keep           │
            │   Informed]     │   Satisfied]     │
            │                 │                 │
            └─────────────────┼─────────────────┘
                              │
                        LOW INFLUENCE
```

| Quadrant                       | Engagement Approach                                    |
| ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| High Influence + High Interest | Manage closely — active collaboration, regular updates |
| High Influence + Low Interest  | Keep satisfied — don't overload, but ensure alignment  |
| Low Influence + High Interest  | Keep informed — newsletters, updates, community        |
| Low Influence + Low Interest   | Monitor — minimum effort, watch for changes            |
