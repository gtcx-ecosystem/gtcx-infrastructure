# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

> The firmographic and behavioral definition of [Organization Name]'s best-fit customer.

---

## What is an ICP?

The Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) defines the type of organization or individual most likely to:

- Experience significant value from the product
- Have the budget and authority to purchase
- Become a long-term, low-churn customer
- Refer others and generate word-of-mouth

The ICP is not a persona (a fictional individual) — it is a profile of an account or organization type. Use it to qualify leads, focus sales effort, and align product development.

## Qualification Framework

Use these questions to qualify or disqualify an account quickly:

### Must-Have (Disqualify if No)

- [ ] Does the organization fall within the target geography?
- [ ] Is the problem we solve genuinely relevant to their current operations?
- [ ] Do they have budget authority or a clear path to approval?
- [ ] Is there urgency — a reason to act now rather than later?

### Nice-to-Have (Increases Priority)

- [ ] Have they tried to solve this before (and failed)?
- [ ] Do they have an internal champion who feels this pain personally?
- [ ] Are they the right size to get value quickly without requiring custom build?
- [ ] Do they have peers or competitors we've already won?
