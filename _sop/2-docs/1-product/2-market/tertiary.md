## What Not to Do

Avoid premature expansion into tertiary markets:

- **Do not** pursue tertiary segments until secondary market is generating consistent revenue
- **Do not** build product features for tertiary segments until primary/secondary demand validates the direction
- **Do not** include tertiary markets in near-term financial projections
