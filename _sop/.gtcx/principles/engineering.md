# [Organization Name] Engineering Principles

_Inherited from [Protocol Partner] — adapted for media/intelligence operations_

---

## Foundational Principles

### 1. Sovereignty-First Design

Every system must enhance rather than circumvent government authority. Intelligence products inform sovereign decision-making; they don't replace it.

### 2. Mathematical Certainty Over Institutional Trust

Verification through cryptographic proof, not reputation. Our indices and intelligence products are auditable, reproducible, and transparent.

### 3. Progressive Enhancement

Systems work at the lowest common denominator (SMS, 2G) and enhance with capability. Content reaches audiences regardless of infrastructure constraints.

### 4. Offline-First Architecture

Core functionality operates without connectivity. Field dispatches, data collection, and verification work in disconnected environments.

### 5. Ubuntu Economics

Individual success strengthens collective prosperity. Our business model creates value for producers, not just extractors.

---

## Intelligence Principles

### 6. Original Research First

We develop primary sources and conduct original analysis. Aggregation is not intelligence.

### 7. Quantify Everything

Vague claims are forbidden. Every assertion includes specific metrics, confidence intervals, or explicit uncertainty acknowledgment.

### 8. Multiple Stakeholder Perspective

Every analysis considers implications for governments, producers, buyers, investors, and development finance. Single-perspective analysis is incomplete.

### 9. Source Transparency

Attribution is mandatory. Confidence levels are indicated. Uncertainty is acknowledged, never hidden.

### 10. Index Integrity

Methodologies are published. Scoring is reproducible. Changes are versioned and explained.

---

## Publishing Principles

### 11. Authority Through Precision

Lead with conclusions, not throat-clearing. Respect reader time. Every sentence earns its place.

### 12. Editorial Independence

Editorial decisions are made by editors. Commercial interests never influence coverage.

### 13. AI-Native Production

Leverage agentic systems for scale while maintaining human editorial judgment at critical gates.

### 14. Cost Efficiency

Institutional-grade content at fractional cost. ~$25/month target for full production pipeline.

### 15. Iterative Quality

[AI System] philosophy: iterate until done. Quality gates, not arbitrary deadlines.

### 17. Entity Separation

### 18. Content Flow Logic

Field stories → Research → News → Execution. Information flows from ground truth to market action.

### 19. Subscription Value

Paid tiers deliver measurably differentiated value. Free tier builds audience; paid tier delivers ROI.

### 20. Community as Verification

[Platform A] peer attestation creates distributed verification. Network effects compound trust.

## Technology Principles

### 21. Boring Technology

PostgreSQL, NestJS, proven tools. Innovation in product, not infrastructure.

### 22. Government-Grade Security

Self-hosted options available. Data sovereignty respected. Audit trails complete.

### 23. API-First Design

Every capability accessible programmatically. Platforms are interfaces to underlying intelligence.

### 25. Git as Memory

## Operational Principles

### 26. Human Gates at Critical Points

AI produces; humans approve. Editorial judgment at release points, not every step.

### 27. Breaking Protocol

Urgent developments have defined response procedures. Speed without chaos.

### 28. Production Cadence

Predictable publishing schedule. Audiences know when to expect content.

### 29. Feedback Loops

Performance metrics inform editorial decisions. What works gets replicated.

### 30. Continuous Improvement

Post-publication review. Error correction. Methodology refinement. Never "done."
